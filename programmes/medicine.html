Bachelor of Nursing Science

Duration: 4 Years (Full-time)

Overview

This programme prepares graduates for professional nursing practice in hospitals, clinics and community settings. It combines theory, simulated practice and clinical placements.

Entry Requirements

  • Matric/Grade 12 (45%+ or 25 points depending on system)
  • Pass in Biology and English (C or equivalent)
  • Medical fitness and police clearance for clinical placement

Modules (Year-by-Year)

Year 1

  • Anatomy & Physiology I
  • Foundations of Nursing Practice
  • Basic Pharmacology
  • Community Health I

Year 2

  • Medical-Surgical Nursing I
  • Pathophysiology
  • Midwifery I
  • Clinical Skills & Simulation

Year 3

  • Mental Health Nursing
  • Pediatrics
  • Community Health II
  • Research Methods

Year 4

  • Advanced Nursing Practice
  • Nursing Leadership & Management
  • Clinical Internship (extended placement)
  • Capstone Project

Career Opportunities

  • Registered Nurse
  • Clinical Nurse Specialist
  • Community Health Nurse
  • Nurse Manager
