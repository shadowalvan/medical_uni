About Windhoek Medical University

Advancing healthcare education for Namibia and beyond

Who We Are

Windhoek Medical University (WMU) is a leading institution dedicated to transforming healthcare education in Africa. Our mission is to develop highly skilled healthcare professionals who can respond to the region’s growing medical needs through innovation, compassion, and excellence.

We combine world-class training, hands-on clinical experience, and community engagement to create graduates who are both academically and practically prepared for the healthcare sector.

Our Mission

At Windhoek Medical University (WMU), we will meet or exceed our learners’ expectations by continuously improving and updating the skills and resources needed for demand driven “Education, Training and Development towards Reflective Health Practice”.

Our Vision

To be a premier African medical university known for excellence in teaching, research, and community health innovation.
