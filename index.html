About Windhoek Medical University

Windhoek Medical University (WMU) is committed to advancing healthcare education, research, and innovation in Africa. We strive to produce highly skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals who will contribute to the well-being of communities across the continent.

Vision

To be a leading medical university in Africa, recognized for excellence in education, research, and healthcare delivery.

Mission

To provide high-quality medical education, conduct innovative research, and engage in community service to improve the health of the people of Africa.

Core Values

  • Excellence
  • Integrity
  • Innovation
  • Compassion
  • Collaboration
