About Windhoek Medical University

Windhoek Medical University (WMU) is committed to advancing healthcare education, research, and innovation in Africa. We strive to produce highly skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals who will contribute to the well-being of communities across the continent.

Vision

  • Help our learners achieve their full potential through quality education, training and recognition of prior learning
  • Work in partnership with our learners and learners and clients to enhance their respect and loyalty
  • Maintain beneficial supplier partnerships to ensure that our needs and expectations for training products and services will be met.
  • Maintain a participatory work ethic, rely on the innate excellence of our staff and provide a training culture in which they excel.
  • Maintain a passion for continuous improvement and continuously improve processes and training related services.

    • Mission

    At Windhoek Medical University (WMU), we will meet or exceed our learners’ expectations by continuously improving and updating the skills and resources needed for demand driven “Education, Training and Development towards Reflective Health Practice”.
